Freshly Squeezed are a 7 piece funk/soul/hip-hop/ska group from South West England. You can catch us playing live covers and original tracks at our regular clubs, pubs, festivals and functions. With a smorgasboard of outstanding musicians, soulful vocals and quick witted MC action we always fill the dancefloor at any event.

Whether we're on stage at an intimate wedding gig or on a festival stage in front of thousands of revellers - you can always expect a set of funk driven dancefloor fillers, reinterpreted funked up arrangements of old classics and virtuoso playing across the board. We strive to give the best show every night, often adding some improvised and fresh off the top funkiness to suit any occasion. 101% freshness guaranteed!
